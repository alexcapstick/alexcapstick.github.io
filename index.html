About me

My name is Alex and I am a PhD student at Imperial College London. My project is focused on using machine learning techniques for the extraction of clinical insights from in-home monitoring data. Within healthcare my research is focused on people living with Dementia.

My interests lie in theoretical machine learning and building new techniques that are applicable to the world of healthcare. This has so far involved researching meta-continual learning, anomaly detection, feature engineering, and designing optimisation methods that allow for predictive models to learn from multiple data sources with different levels of data quality. Until now, my work has focused on applying these techniques to the analysis of in-home monitoring data to predict urinary tract infections and to learn participant behaviours.
